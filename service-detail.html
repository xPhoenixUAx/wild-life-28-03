Common signs

How raccoon activity often shows up around a home.

  • Heavy footstep sounds above ceilings at night
  • Torn shingles, lifted soffits, or damaged roof vents
  • Strong odor near attic zones, dormers, or wall lines
  • Insulation displacement, droppings, and nesting debris

Property concerns

Why roofline and attic issues can escalate quickly.

  • Widened openings can invite squirrels, rats, and birds later
  • Contaminated insulation can spread odor through living spaces
  • Repeated denning can affect soffits, fascia, and vent assemblies
  • Nearby food access can increase repeat nighttime visits

Preventive focus

Follow-up measures often discussed after raccoon removal.

01

Seal upper access points

Roofline gaps, loose fascia sections, and damaged vents often need reinforced closure.

02

Address attractants

Trash storage, pet food, and outdoor shelter zones can affect repeat activity around the property.

03

Plan cleanup and repairs

Sanitation, insulation replacement, and odor management may be important after attic nesting activity.
