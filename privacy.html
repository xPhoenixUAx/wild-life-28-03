1. Introduction

Wildlife Control Connect ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Wildlife Control Connect is an independent, family-owned informational and referral platform designed to help homeowners understand wildlife-related issues, explore service categories, and connect with independent third-party providers focused on humane animal removal and property protection.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with the Service.

By using this website, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

  • Submit contact or inquiry forms

  • Subscribe to updates or communications
  • Communicate with us directly

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • General location (city/ZIP code)

  • Any additional information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address and approximate location

This information is used in an aggregated and non-identifiable way where possible.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality and understand user behavior.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
